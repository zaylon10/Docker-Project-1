Deep Dish Brownies

Prep Time

60 mins

Servings

9

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and grease an 8 inch square pan.
  2. In a large bowl, blend melted butter,sugar and vanilla.
  3. beat in eggs one at a time
  4. SPread the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 40 to 45 minutes or until brownies begin to pull away from the sides of the pan.
  6. Let brownies cool, then cut into squares. Enjoy!

Tip

Aluminum foil can be used to keep food moist, cook it evenly and make clean-up easier.
